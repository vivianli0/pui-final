EVOLUTIONARY HISTORY OF LIFE ON EARTH

from 4.6 billion years ago to now

This a paragraph about the Big Bang and how Earth was created. There is so much we don’t know, so this website will be as accurate as it can, as of 2023. Let’s dive into 4.6 million years of geological history and see how life grew from dead matters into the vibrant world we have today!

4.6 billion years ago

Earth was born, and it was very hot.

The Earth was formed out of a mixture of dust and gas around the young sun. It grew larger thanks to countless collisions between dust particles, asteroids, and other growing planets, including one last giant impact that threw enough rock, gas, and dust into space to form the moon.
